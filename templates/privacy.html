Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information you provide at registration, such as your name and email address.
  • Expense data you enter into the app, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Basic usage data such as login timestamps and session activity, used to maintain account security.

We do not collect payment information, government identifiers, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically:

  • Your account details are used to authenticate you and personalise your experience.
  • Your expense data is used to display your spending history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • Usage data is used to detect unusual activity and keep your account secure.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load the typefaces used in the interface. Google may log font requests in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We do not share your personal or financial data with any third parties for marketing, analytics, or any other commercial purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly.
